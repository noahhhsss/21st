TROLL

21ST CENTURY

Plot

The plot follows the lives of a young person who goes online to become an infamous troll. He immediately becomes obsessed with causing disruption and controversy on social media sites by making harsh comments and falsely accused posts. He conceals his identity behind a fictitious name, experiencing empowerment in mystery as he causes chaos on celebrities, influencers and everyday life of people for pleasure and self-validation. As the troll's popularity develops on the computer, His online identity affects his real life, and he struggles to distinguish himself from the screen. Online activity begins to spill over into everyday life. Through the story's turning point, the coordinated response targets the troll with the same hate he had gave. When he has to admit the harm he has caused to others as well as himself, he learns a valuable lesson about empathy and consequences. The story goes into the psychological consequences of online activity, as well as the battle for repentance and change. At the end of it all, the protagonist understands that his actions were pointless. The story focuses on accountability, self-awareness and the need for kindness in today's digital world.

Settings

- Quezon City
- TAYO Networking Inc. Main Headquarters, #1, Balimbing Street
- TAYO Development Corporation Village, Quezon City

Symbolism

The mask symbolizes the anonymity of the internet, allowing individuals to hide their true selves while projecting a different persona. It represents both freedom and danger: the freedom to express oneself without fear of judgment, but also the danger of using that anonymity to harm others. In the context of Troll, the mask can be seen as a barrier that shields trolls from accountability, enabling toxic behaviors that have real consequences for the victims.

Characters

The characters in the story Kiko, Jessy, and Ronny are the three identities of the main character in "Troll." Kiko is snarky and has a foul mouth when talking, Ronny is cheerful and happy and he always uses slang phrases like "charot" in his conversations, and Jessica is always dramatic. The setting in the story is At TAYO Networking Inc. Main Headquarters, #1 Balimbing Street, WE Development Corporation Village Quezon City house. The conflict in this story is primarily about the person vs. self because the protagonist's opponent/enemy is his ambition to be wealthy, even though he feels terrible for being a media troll.

Conflict

THE CONFLICT is about the fight between a person (the victim) and online trolls. Trolls are people who post hurtful or mean comments online to make others feel bad. The main problem happens when the victim feels sad, scared, or angry because of the troll's actions, The story shows how this person struggles to deal with the trolls while trying to stay strong and not let the negativity affect their life. It’s a battle of emotions between the hurt caused by the trolls and the courage to overcome it

Theme

The theme of this short story is cyberbullying, online harrassment, struggles of finding a job, and spreaing misinformation. Also, the beginning of the story shows the struggle of the main character to fin a meanigdul job and ed ups becoming a troll for a Networking company calle TAYO Networking Inc. Furthermore, the story emphasizes how easy it is to spread missninfomation and negativity through social media.

Lessons

*Har Work and perserverance Pay Off -Despite frustrations, the protagonist pushes through their struggles at work and life, which ultimately leads to opportuniities. This demonstrates that perseverance in tough situations can open new doors. *Adaptability and growth -The Story highlights the importance of adapting to situations and learning from experiences. The Character grows by exploring oppotunities outside their comfort zone, showing that growth often happens through challenges. The Value of Networking and Opportunity seeking - the protagonist's eventual involvement with "TAYO Networking Inc." Symbolizes how seeking out and embracing new opportunities can lead to better outcomes. It emphasizes the importance of staying open to possibilities and making connections.
